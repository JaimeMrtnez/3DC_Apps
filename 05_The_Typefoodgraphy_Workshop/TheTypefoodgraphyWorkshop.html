The Typefoodgraphy Workshop

Welcome to The Typefoodgraphy Workshop!

With this mini app, you can create your own type design in a quick and easy way.
First, type in the text you want. The maximum length is 10 characters. If you need longer text, simply generate more files. Then try to manipulate it by changing the base font and the size, or the funniest, click on different parts of the canvas to see how it deforms and takes on a new personality. When you're happy with the result you can save a file ready to work with your food printer. Have a good time and Bon Appetit!
